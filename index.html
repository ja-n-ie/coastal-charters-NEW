Everything’s Better
on the Water

Coastal Charters is a Vancouver Island owned business with nearly 20 years of experience in the marine and hospitality industries. We are a premier recreational sportfishing and marine adventure company with a mission to provide high caliber products and services for our guests that are exciting, safe and value based. With the pristine waters and wilderness of Tofino, British Columbia as the backdrop you can expect to not only enjoy amazing angling opportunities but also experience breathtaking sightings of wildlife such as orcas, grey whales, bears, wolves, eagles and more!

We believe in social responsibility and that a strong community is as important as a strong crew. That’s why we work closely with non-profit organizations and such as the Take a Hike Youth at Risk Foundation and Kids 4 Kids donating our time and resources to offer vocational training, mentoring and leadership to at risk youth.

Under the Surface

The West Coast is world famous for its Pacific salmon runs and recreational sport fishery that’s available to eager anglers flocking to try their hand, or knuckles, at landing some monster salmon. Wild Chinook (King) Coho (Silver) Sockeye, Pink and Chum (Dogfish) are all available through varying months of the year in Tofino. The big banks and fishing holes around the coast offer up outstanding bottom fishing for giant halibut, snapper (yellow eye) lingcod and rockfish. Offshore fishing is available for albacore tuna when in season and weather cooperates. Pacific albacore are prized for their meat and texture and considered to be sushi grade (meaning they can be eaten raw).

The Rig

Kick back in first class and relax in style on the 27 foot Grady White Sailfish you will be boarding. The sailfish boasts twin 200 horsepower four stroke Yamaha outboard engines getting us to where we need to be quietly and efficiently. There is ample storage and seating on board the Sailfish and with a 9’6 beam the open back deck is the perfect stage for battling monster salmon and halibut!
